Singapore

When we arrived in Singapore we realised just how bad the coronavirus outbreak was. This was the first place that we were regularly getting our temperatures checked and hotels and businesses were starting to take more precautions.

Even though we had just come from the Australian summer nothing could prepare us for the heat of Singapore. As soon as you stepped outside you were sweating and walking up four or five steps always felt like a mission.
