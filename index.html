Now it's all over

Although things didn't turn out as planned we still had a fantastic time and if I could go back to January knowing that the trip would be cut short I would still choose to go.

However, the whole thing was very strange. We felt like we were in our own little bubble trying to run away from the virus. From the east coast of Australia onwards our daily routine evolved around checking for Covid-19 updates and deciding whether our future travel plans were still viable and if not rushing to make new ones.

We felt like we were winning, we'd managed to avoid the lockdowns and restrictions for so long but eventually it all became too much and we had to admit defeat and head back to the UK.

Since we’ve been home I've spent my time learning web development. I’m really enjoying it and I’m proud of what I’ve managed to achieve. I’ve built this site from scratch and you can check out the rest of my work here. I often wonder if I’d ever have found the time to develop these skills if it hadn’t been for Covid-19.
