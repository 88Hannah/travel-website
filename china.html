China

The first stop on our trip was China. Although we had put a lot of thought into our route this first stop was mainly because this was as far as we could get on the upgrade voucher that we had from our air miles.

When we arrived everything was so exciting and new; a big change from the UK.

We only made one stop in China. Our initial plan was to come back again later on in the trip for about a month to explore it properly but unfortunately this never happened.
